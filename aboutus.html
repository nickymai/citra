“In a world full of social judgments based on choices and principles, we believe that self-expression should not be frowned upon because only then can we tap into an inclusive world full of unique creativity, desires, and passions” Citra is a non-alcoholic beer that stands for an equitable society in which everyone is free to do what they want and express themselves. You may pick your identity from a variety of labels that serve as a social accessory. Everyone has the right to be themselves. #staysober
